About Purple

MEET
HARRY

Purple is the private atelier of Harry — an Anthurium obsessive based in Saigon, Vietnam. What began with a single velvet leaf grew into a climate-controlled room where the rarest aroids are raised like living jewellery.

Held at a steady 26°C and 70–85% humidity, every specimen is hand-selected for vein, velvet and form — a veinclusive purple selection where love and luxury grow in every leaf.
